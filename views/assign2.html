Our assignment this week was to play around with the laser cutter and vinyl cutter and to make a cardboard press-fit kit using the vinyl cutter. I spent the majority of my time working with the vinyl cutter because I have worked a lot in Adobe Illustrator and was really excited by the idea of being help to turn illustrator files into laptop stickers

Before and in class we learned how to use Fusion 360. I've used some other 3D graphics programs and I'm not sure I like this one as much, it doesn't feel very user friendly. I really like 123d Design for quick 3D mock-ups like we were doing. None the less I played around in fusion 360 and made some fun shapes

I then spent (a very very very short amount of time) trying to replicate some of the press fit kit items we had build in class, again using fusion360. My initial print did not work, and I'm going to work more this week to get more comfortable with fusion 360 and the laser cutter. I will add more to this section as my exploration continues :)
