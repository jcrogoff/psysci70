Who are we?

CareerPeak is a Career Discovery Platform for non-STEM students

Why does Career Peak Need to Exist?

Since when did our society decide only degrees in STEM (science, technology, engineering, mathematics) are valuable. This is a major problem when you consider that over 80% of students are in non-STEM majors and 60% of non-STEM are women and half are non-white. I talked to 50 recruiters who said they are committed to increasing the diversity in their companies. How do they expect to do that without tapping into the largest pool of diverse candidates. So enter CareerPeak, a career discovery platform that helps non-STEM students find their value in the job market and helps recruiters find diverse candidates they value. Unlike current recruiter facing solutions that expect students to navigate the recruitment progress on their own, CareerPeak is a student facing platform that provides a guided career finding experience. Students answer questions, connect with company advisors, explore careers and eventually create a targeted resume for the jobs they decide to pursue.
