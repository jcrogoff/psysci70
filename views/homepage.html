FINAL PROJECT

Digesting the News

For my final project I am going to create a sculpture which responds to the current news of the day and adjusts itself based on the given information.

Brainstorming

To start off I wanted to think a little about what kinds of things were rapidly changing in this environment as well as what pieces of news I would be most interestested in reflecting in the artwork. While there are many possible pieces I'd like to include I settled on three things to start -- money, food, and medical supplies. I decided to start with money. The stock market is EXTREAMLY volitile right now, which I think makes it a great subject for this type of responsive artwork. Thus, the first piece I've decided to create is a piece that responds to daily information about the stock market. (I could of course make this hourly as well but I deicded generally to work with the idea of the sculpture changing once at noon every day -- I can adjust this later throughout the project if necessary).

After drawing up a few different designs I decided one I liked is an accordian crumpling of a bill. I could later exapnd this to mutilple billls and sort of use the response as a bar graph (with the strech size of the bill being the maximum size on the bar.) For now I am working with a $1 bill in case I break it... but I'd like to use a fake $100 dollar bill if its possible to get one of these days.
